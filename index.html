Meet the Founder

Meet the Founder

Based in Manhattan, our founder built Supramar to serve a small circle of clients seeking a private, high-standard fitness environment in the heart of New York City. At the heart of our club is our founder, whose journey from elite athletics to private fitness consulting shaped the personal approach that defines everything we do.

A former professional water polo goalkeeper, he learned early the value of discipline, resilience, and consistency — lessons that continue to inform his philosophy today. After completing his education in economics and finance in New York, he combined his passion for sport, fitness, and human performance with a deep understanding of how to build meaningful, lasting client relationships.

Over the years, he has quietly built a trusted reputation working with executives, entrepreneurs, and professionals who value discretion, results, and an environment where they are truly known. His approach is simple: care for each member like you would for family — with attention, honesty, and consistency.

For him, success is not measured in memberships sold, but in the years of trust earned with each individual who walks through the door.
